Trit Language Translator

Press keys 1, 2, and 3 with short or long presses simultaneously to type.

To first learn, when attempting to type a letter, hold down the key(s) which need a 2 (long-click) and simultaneously click any which need a short click. Then let go of all keys.

The encodings were based on the relative frequencies of each letter in the english dictionary, and the ease of typing each 3trit combination, from easiest to most difficult.
